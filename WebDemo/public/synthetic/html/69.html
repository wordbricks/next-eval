Anime conventions abroad

The best way to experience anime culture overseas is by attending an international anime convention. You can purchase a convention pass for $20 per day in smaller local events, or $100 per day in major international events. Convention passes include access to panels, screenings, and exclusive merchandise. Usage is valid for the duration of the convention. If you need more exclusive merchandise, you can purchase additional items for varying prices. A convention pass is activated upon entry to the convention hall, participation in a panel, or purchase of merchandise at the event.

If you’re watching anime in a country where Crunchyroll isn’t accessible, you cannot stream your favorite shows.

Crunchyroll Premium upgrade

You're eligible for the Crunchyroll Premium upgrade if you’re a current Crunchyroll Premium member or if you subscribe to Crunchyroll Premium and are on a Mega Fan or Ultimate Fan plan. Crunchyroll Premium tier upgrade will be applied within 14 days and will apply for a year aligned to membership date. Once you reach the anniversary of the relevant membership year, we'll notify you that this offer is expiring and you'll be moved to the relevant tier based on your previous year’s viewing.

Ending your subscription

If you cancel your Crunchyroll Premium Subscription, you will not receive a partial refund for the remainder of your month. If you have any digital manga, figures or merchandise add-ons attached to your subscription, you will have to pay any remaining amount owing as a one-off payment.

Modifications to your subscription

Your Crunchyroll Premium subscription may change (including price and content). With no commitment, you can change your subscription once a month or cancel. If you cancel, just pay out your figures, merchandise or digital manga in full.
