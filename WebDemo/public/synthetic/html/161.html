Choosing between different substances can be difficult without the proper resources, so let the educators at Khan Academy simplify the process. Simply choose the substances you're interested in from our detailed list, and we'll handle the rest.

Our advanced substance comparison tool allows you to analyze multiple substances, so you can quickly see their differences. Compare properties, reactions, structures, atomic masses, and even safety information. View explanations and advantages and disadvantages for each substance, and compare not just theoretical data but also experimental results and real-world applications. You can compare related substances, or compare different isotopes or phases of the same element.

Our comparator isn't just about determining which substance is superior, it's about determining which substance is most suitable for your needs. Compare over sixty easy-to-understand data points, and discover what our instructors and students think of each substance on your list.
