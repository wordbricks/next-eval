What is a fantasy novel?

A fantasy novel is a work of fiction set in an imaginary world, often involving magic, mythical creatures, and epic quests. It explores themes of good versus evil, courage, and the power of imagination, offering readers an escape into realms beyond the ordinary.

While reading can be a relaxing pre-sleep activity, the type of material and reading device can affect sleep quality. Choose physical books over screens and avoid engaging content to ensure a restful night. It's important to monitor your reading habits and adjust as needed.
