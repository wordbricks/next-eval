    Always On

    These trackers are essential for the platform to operate and cannot be disabled in our infrastructure. They are generally only activated in response to actions initiated by you that constitute a service request, such as configuring your data settings, signing in, or completing forms. You can configure your browser to block or notify you about these trackers, but certain sections of the platform will then be inaccessible. Disabling these metrics will hinder our ability to track and improve user growth.

    These tools enhance user efficiency and streamline workflows within the platform. They can be developed internally or integrated from third-party providers. Disabling these tools may limit certain functionalities and reduce overall productivity.

    Monetization Strategies
