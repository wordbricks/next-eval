Protect My Health Data

When you access our website, we use tracking technologies on your browser to gather health information. The health information collected might relate to you, your health conditions or your devices, and is primarily used to improve the site's functionality and to deliver a more tailored health experience. Under the Health Information Portability and Accountability Act of 1996 ("HIPAA"), US residents have the right to control the "sharing" of personal health information (transfers or disclosures to other organizations or third parties for research or other valuable purposes) we collect about them ("Health Data") as described in our Privacy & Health Data Policy. You can find our Privacy & Health Data Policy by clicking on the link below. We respect your health privacy and aim to honor your choices regarding your health data. Regulations from the US Department of Health setting forth exactly how organizations are required to comply with HIPAA are continuously evolving, and we will monitor those developments closely. Presented below are options for US residents who want to opt-out of certain sharing of health data that we may have collected from you as you have interacted with our website, our apps, or otherwise used our services. We may update this information as guidance from the US Department of Health is clarified and finalized. Generally, sharing of health data occurs (1) because of tracking technologies and similar tools on your desktop browser or your mobile device, or (2) from our internal systems. To exercise your choice with respect to tracking technologies or other tools, please use the button below. To inform us about your choices with respect to other sharing, please submit a Health Data Request using the link below.
