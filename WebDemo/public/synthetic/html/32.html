      Popular Fitness Questions

      What is the best exercise for abs?

      Effective ab workouts typically last 15 to 20 minutes, targeting upper, lower, and oblique muscles. Crunches, planks, and leg raises are beneficial. Aim for 3 sets of 15-20 reps, focusing on form. Consistency and varied exercises yield optimal results, enhancing core strength and definition.

      What are the benefits of yoga?

      Yoga improves flexibility, strength, and balance, typically practiced for 30 to 60 minutes. Regular sessions reduce stress and enhance mental clarity. Various styles cater to different fitness levels, promoting overall well-being and mindfulness. Consistency is key for sustained benefits.

      How long should I hold a plank?

      Hold a plank for 30 to 60 seconds, engaging core muscles for stability. Repeat 3-5 times with short breaks. Proper form prevents injury and maximizes effectiveness. Gradually increase duration as strength improves, enhancing core endurance and posture.
