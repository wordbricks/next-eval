    Nutrition Choices Center

    When you access any nutrition portal, it could save or access data on your device, primarily through storage mechanisms. This data could relate to your dietary habits, your preferences, or your device, and it's mainly utilized to ensure the portal functions correctly. The data typically doesn't directly identify you, but it can offer a more tailored nutrition guidance experience. Given our commitment to your data protection, you have the option to decline certain types of data collection. Explore the various section titles to learn more and modify our standard configurations. However, disabling certain data collection methods might affect your experience on the portal and the resources we can provide.

    Customize Data Preferences

    Essential Data Collection

    Always Enabled

    These data collection methods are vital for the portal's operation and cannot be disabled within our infrastructure. They are generally activated in response to actions you take that constitute a request for resources, such as configuring your data protection settings, signing in, or completing questionnaires. You can configure your device to block or notify you about these methods, but certain sections of the portal may then be inaccessible. These methods do not retain any personally identifiable details.

    Analytics Data Collection

    These insights enable us to track user engagement and content preferences, allowing us to evaluate and enhance the effectiveness of our platform. They assist us in identifying which articles are the most and least engaging and observe how users navigate through the portal. All data gathered by these insights is combined and kept confidential. Disabling these insights will prevent us from knowing when you have accessed our portal, and we will be unable to assess its effectiveness.

    Personalized Recommendations

    These recommendations might be implemented within our portal by our nutrition partners. They could be utilized by those organizations to develop a summary of your dietary needs and display relevant content on other platforms. They do not retain directly identifiable data, but depend on specifically recognizing your device and network connection. Disabling these recommendations will result in less customized content.

    Enhanced Features

    These features allow the portal to deliver improved performance and customization. They could be implemented by us or by external service providers whose tools we've incorporated into our pages. Disabling these features might cause certain functionalities to operate incorrectly or not at all.
