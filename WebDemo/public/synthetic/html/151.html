              Data Protection

              When browsing any job board, data is collected to enhance your experience. This data, often in the form of cookies, helps tailor job recommendations and improve site functionality. It may include details about your search history, preferences, and device. While this data doesn't directly identify you, it allows for a more personalized job search. We value your privacy, and you have the option to manage cookie settings. Explore the different categories below to learn more and customize your preferences. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect your ability to fully utilize all site features and services.

              Required Cookies

              Always Enabled

              These cookies are essential for the website's core functionality and cannot be disabled. They are typically activated in response to your actions, such as setting privacy preferences, logging in, or completing application forms. You can configure your browser to block or notify you about these cookies, but doing so may prevent certain parts of the site from functioning correctly. These cookies do not store any personally identifiable data.

              Analytics and Performance Cookies

              These trackers help us analyze user engagement and traffic patterns to optimize our platform's effectiveness. They show us which sections are most and least engaging and track how users navigate the platform. All data gathered by these trackers is compiled and kept confidential. Disabling these trackers will prevent us from knowing when you accessed our platform, hindering our ability to assess its performance.

              Essential Trackers

              These trackers allow the platform to offer improved features and customization. They could be implemented by us or by external vendors whose functionalities we've integrated into our pages. Disabling these trackers might cause certain features to malfunction or become unavailable.

              Advertising Trackers

              These trackers might be installed on our platform by our marketing collaborators. They could be utilized by those entities to create a profile of your preferences and display relevant job postings on other platforms. They do not directly retain personal data, but depend on uniquely identifying your device and browser. Disabling these trackers will result in less personalized job recommendations.
