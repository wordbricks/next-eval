Welcome

A top attraction if you visit NYC! Their coffee is incredibly rich with a bold taste, particularly if you appreciate strong coffee. However, it's not too intense. I suggest avoiding the regular latte if you prefer a milder coffee flavor, as it has a robust coffee taste. I particularly enjoyed the caramel latte, which had a great mix of coffee and sweetness. My colleague also tried their vanilla latte with an extra shot, which looked amazing. They also offer pastries around $8-12, and I've heard they're delicious, so I'll definitely try them next time.

Urban Adventures

I chose to visit this spot during my city exploration today. This tour location is situated in the Downtown/Waterfront district, and the entire area appears historically significant. The meeting point is inside the Grand Central Terminal's main hall, and I found convenient parking nearby. Upon arrival, I was met with a friendly greeting and a helpful guide. As I arrived right on time, there were only a few people gathered. I booked a walking tour of Historic Landmarks with a focus on architecture. They offered discounts for seniors, which was appreciated. From the first stop, I could really appreciate the history. I was pleased that the tour wasn't overshadowed by modern anecdotes or irrelevant stories. If it had been, it wouldn't be a good tour. The historical facts were very interesting and integrated well with the sights. However, if I could suggest one thing, I would recommend a slightly longer duration next time, even if that means a higher price. The tour felt a little too short for me. Overall, excellent city tour!
