WHAT IS ANIME AND HOW IS IT ENJOYED?

Similar to many online communities, we utilize anime references (small visual cues or phrases) and other cultural expressions within the Forum (collectively known as Anime Moments, unless specified otherwise), including character references, iconic scenes, fan art, forum avatars, and user signatures.

Community-created Anime Moments

Community-created Anime Moments are generated by our users (sometimes with the help of external artists) and are used to enhance your experience on the Forum and its features and to support community engagement.

Official Anime Moments

Certain official sources may share their Anime Moments on our platform and utilize them to identify your preferences when you browse the Forum and when you visit other anime-related websites or online platforms. These official sources gather and utilize this data according to their own content policies. Official Anime Moments enable specific content or functionalities, and promotions, to be offered on the Forum.

Popular Anime Series

This board uses the following categories to organize anime discussions:

Action/Adventure Anime:Discuss anime with intense battles, daring quests, and thrilling storylines. Remember to use spoiler tags!

Comedy/Slice of Life Anime:Share your thoughts on lighthearted and humorous anime focusing on everyday life.

Fantasy/Sci-Fi Anime:Explore magical worlds, futuristic technologies, and alternate realities in anime. Discuss world-building and character abilities.

Romance/Drama Anime:Delve into emotional relationships, personal struggles, and character growth in anime.

Anime Viewing and Discussion Cookies:Data gathered under this category can also be used to suggest and deliver personalized anime content, such as new episodes and related discussions.

Cosplay Suggestion and Creation Cookies:These Cookies are used to collect data about your anime interests, your use of the forum, your preferences, and your interaction with cosplay across platforms and devices for the purpose of delivering interest-based cosplay content on the forum and on third-party sites. Third-party sites and services also use interest-based Cosplay Cookies to deliver content, including cosplay suggestions relevant to your interests on the forum and third-party services. If you reject these Cookies, you may see general cosplay suggestions that may be less relevant to you.

Forum Interaction Cookies:These Cookies are set by forum platforms on the forum to enable you to share content with your fellow anime fans and groups. Forum platforms have the ability to track your online activity outside of the forum. This may impact the content and discussions you see on other services you visit.

We and third parties may associate Anime History And Analytics Cookies, Anime Preference Cookies, Anime Viewing, Discussion Cookies, and Reporting, Cosplay Suggestion, Creation and Reporting Cookies, and Forum Interaction Cookies with other information we have about you.
