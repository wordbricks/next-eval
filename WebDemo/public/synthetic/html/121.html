    Finding the right tech job is a crucial step to demonstrate value for the companies and leaders who need it. Whether you're actively searching for your next opportunity or simply exploring the market, here's some useful advice and resources.

    When is the next Tech Job Fair?

    The next Tech Job Fair in London is held annually in the third week of September. Save the date: the next event will be on Wednesday, 17th September 2025. It's important to note that various cities host tech job fairs on different dates and with their own specific focuses.

    What is a Tech Job Fair? A quick overview

    Tech Job Fairs have origins tracing back to the late 1990s with the rise of the internet. This was initially a way for companies to meet potential employees, or 'future talent'. Software engineers were often given the opportunity to interview, and they might receive offers on the spot for their skills. The tradition has grown, especially after the dot-com boom began in 2000, integrating the global focus on recruiting talent. By the early 21st century, it became the widely recognized event for tech recruitment we see today in the UK. It's a fantastic chance to thank engineers and tech professionals for everything they contribute!

Find roles that match your skills and experience, from entry-level positions to senior leadership roles. Browse our listings to discover companies with innovative cultures and competitive benefits. Whether you're seeking a remote position or a role in a vibrant city, we have options to suit your preferences. Start your journey towards a rewarding tech career by exploring our current openings.

    Strategies for Landing Your Dream Tech Job (Anytime)

    Eager to secure a tech role that aligns with your career aspirations, regardless of the timing?

    • Reflect on your technical skills and interests - what technologies are you passionate about? Aligning your job search with your expertise demonstrates your commitment.
    • Pay attention to industry trends! Companies often highlight the skills they need in job descriptions and company announcements.
    • Sometimes the best career move isn't a new job, but professional development or networking opportunities that enhance your skills.
    • Evaluate your preferred work environment - do you thrive in a fast-paced startup, a large corporation, or a remote setting?
    • When unsure, invest in yourself! A relevant online course, certification, or conference can significantly boost your marketability.

    Ongoing Methods to Advance Your Tech Career

    Don't postpone your career goals; start your job search today. Consistent effort throughout the year leads to success. Consider updating your resume, practicing interview skills, networking with industry professionals, or exploring online courses to enhance your qualifications. Remember, the most valuable investment you can make is in your own professional development!
