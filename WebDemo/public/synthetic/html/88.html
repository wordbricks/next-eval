SLEEPTRAINING 101: Surviving Naptime

In this introductory blog, we explore the world of baby sleep, sharing different experiences, tips, and tricks as we go. The fundamental challenges of establishing a sleep schedule, dealing with night wakings, and managing sleep regressions are discussed. From our home: its cozy nursery, quiet corners, and familiar spaces, to parks and playgrounds, to family vacations, stories will range from quick anecdotes to longer, more reflective pieces. Through personal experiences, expert advice, and community insights, we explore the ups and downs of parenting, as well as those moments of pure joy. We move from sleepless nights to peaceful mornings, fussy babies to content toddlers, chaotic days to calm evenings, considering how even the smallest victory can feel as monumental as climbing Mount Everest. Both new and experienced parents are welcome. Always.

This blog is designed to help new parents navigate the overwhelming world of infancy. Drawing from personal experiences and expert advice, the blog complements traditional parenting books by focusing on real-life challenges and triumphs. Relevant for those who are sleep-deprived and covered in spit-up, the blog emphasizes principles that empower individuals to take more control of their sanity: investing in coffee, building a support network, taking naps when possible, and making the most of fleeting moments of peace. Through a series of humorous anecdotes and interviews - with pediatricians, lactation consultants, sleep experts, and other parents - readers gain knowledge and strategies related to topics including effective swaddling and soothing techniques, mastering the art of the diaper change, decoding baby cries, translating developmental milestones into everyday activities, and building a strong parent-child bond. Additional topics include baby-led weaning and toddler tantrums, and strategies for cultivating a happy and healthy family life. Guest contributors for the Winter 2024 season include: Award-winning author of "Happiest Baby on the Block," Dr. Harvey Karp; renowned pediatrician and author of "Your Baby's First Year," Dr. Penelope Leach; CEO of a leading organic baby food company, Sarah Smith; National Parenting Expert and distinguished professor of Child Development at UCLA, Patricia Greenfield; SVP and Chief Wellness Officer of Johnson & Johnson, Dr. Joaquin Duato; Stanford professors and parents, Carol Dweck and Jo Boaler; and author and founder of Mindful Parenting Initiative, Susan Kaiser Greenland. Prior guests have included author and Facebook COO, Sheryl Sandberg; Chief Education Officer at Sesame Workshop, Dr. Rosemarie Truglio; 3-time Olympic gold medalist and mother of three, Kerri Walsh Jennings; former CEO of LEGO Group, Jorgen Vig Knudstorp; and CNN contributor, author, and social entrepreneur, Van Jones. The idea for this blog emerged from the author's reflections on 5 years of parenting, teaching, mentorship, and deep personal experiences spanning the gamut of approaches to raising children - academic research, personal anecdotes, conceiving of and leading parent support groups, building family and community networks, launching and financing childcare solutions, building business models to advance real-world applications of cutting-edge science, and seeing through research-based companies to success. For this blog, readers will be encouraged to share their own stories, participate actively in the comments section, and complete reflection exercises focused on parenting topics as they relate to one's own family needs and personal aspirations.
