Your Security

When browsing our platform, we collect data to enhance your experience, primarily through location services. This data includes your general area, preferred neighborhoods, and housing interests. We use this information to personalize your search results, recommend relevant properties, and improve our platform's functionality. Your location data is anonymized and aggregated to protect your privacy. You have the option to disable location services at any time, but this may limit certain features and personalization options.
Terms of Service

Essential Features

Always Enabled

These features are crucial for the platform's operation and cannot be disabled. They include core functionalities such as property search, user authentication, and secure transactions. Disabling these features would prevent you from using the platform effectively. These features do not collect any personally identifiable information beyond what is necessary for their operation.

Personalization Features

These features allow the platform to provide a more tailored and relevant experience. They may be enabled by us or by third-party services that enhance our platform. If you disable these features, some aspects of the platform may not function optimally or provide personalized recommendations.

Analytics Trackers

These trackers help us understand user behavior and improve our platform. They show us which listings are viewed most often and how users navigate the site. All data collected is anonymous and aggregated. Disabling these trackers prevents us from optimizing your experience based on site usage.

Personalized Ad Trackers

These trackers may be placed on our site by advertising partners. They help create a profile of your interests to show you relevant property listings on other platforms. They do not store personal information directly but identify your browser and device. Disabling these trackers will result in less relevant property recommendations.
