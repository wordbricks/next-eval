How to buy dog treats at Petco

  • Find a type of dog food you like
    Then click Add to cart. (Just like you normally would.)
  • Select your dog's breed
    Select from various breeds (common pets for home or farm) or exotic (a pet that requires special care). We also have rescues (no adoption fee!), as well as temporary foster options.
  • Select your pet type
    We offer common dog breeds, rare cat breeds, and exotic bird species in several colors (yellow, blue, and red). If youd like a rare breed, select them for an additional $75.

Enhance your pet's life by combining premium food with interactive toys

  • Interactive toys and premium food make a great combinationfor active pets or relaxed companions. You can apply both to any pet at checkout.
  • Premium food helps improve the health of your pet (like their coat or energy levels).
  • Interactive toys feature a stimulating design intended to reduce boredom by helping the pet transition from rest to play.
